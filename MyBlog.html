Introductions

Written by Tyler Ross on August 2, 2015.
Hello! My name is Tyler and the purpose of this blog is to both develop and put into writing my many thoughts across whatever projects or technologies I am using/learning/building. Much of what I will be doing is either sharing some cool tip I discovered or... complaining about some small language/design feature that sucks.

In order to understand more about me, I am currently a bike shop mechanic and am finishing my Mathematics degree with an emphasis in Optimization. Besides loving (real) analysis, I do have a CS background and have experience with Artificial Intelligence, Data Structures, web development and algorithm design and I hope to grow this knowledge and share it with the world. If you have any problems with anything I have written or I missed something you think worthwhile (which will happen), please please go to Contact me and let me know.

While I plan to keep this blog about tidbits of foray into the wide world Computer Science and software development, it will be difficult not to talk about some bike things and I promise bike metaphors will be kept at a minimum. Bounjour!
